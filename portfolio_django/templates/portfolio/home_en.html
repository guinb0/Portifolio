I'm a data intern at CGU (Federal Comptroller General's Office), Brazil's federal internal control agency. I perform user journey testing, cross-referencing different databases for decision support using SQL Server, Python, and R.

I'm pursuing a degree in Computer Science and my work focuses on the data field, with emphasis on skills: Power BI, Python, SQL Server, R. However, I'm also familiar with web development, where I've worked on several projects, including e-commerce websites and my professional portfolio.

Besides my internship, I volunteer at São Paulo Social Observatory, an institution that monitors São Paulo's political activities, from parliamentary sessions to public tenders. At the Observatory, I work with data extraction and processing for database integration.
